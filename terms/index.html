01
Overview

These Terms and Conditions govern all reservations made directly through Lake City Flats, LLC ("LCF," "we," "us") at lakecityflats.com. By completing a booking you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these terms on behalf of yourself and all guests in your party.

Lake City Flats operates 26 curated short-term rental properties across Salt Lake City, Utah and Detroit, Michigan. Direct bookings are processed securely through our booking partner Hospitable via Stripe.

02
Reservations & Payment

A reservation is confirmed upon receipt of full payment unless otherwise agreed in writing. Payment is processed at time of booking via Stripe. We accept all major credit cards.

  • The guest who completes the reservation is the primary guest and is responsible for all parties in the property.
  • The primary guest must be at least 21 years of age and present during the stay.
  • Pricing displayed on our site is per-night and reflects the total nightly rate before applicable taxes and fees, which are shown at checkout.
  • Reservations are not transferable to third parties.
Why book direct?
Booking directly with us saves you approximately 15% compared to Airbnb — you skip the OTA service fee entirely. The same Stripe-secured payment, the same property, a lower total.
03
Cancellation Policy

We understand plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both guests and our small team.

All cancellation deadlines are measured from 12:00 PM local time on the day of check-in (Mountain Time for SLC properties; Eastern Time for Detroit properties). Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

In the event of a government-mandated travel restriction, natural disaster, or other documented force majeure that directly prevents occupancy, we will work with you to reschedule or issue a full credit. Contact us at contact@lakecityflats.com as soon as possible.

04
Check-in & Check-out
  • Check-in: 4:00 PM local time. Early check-in may be available upon request, subject to cleaning schedules.
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM local time. Late check-out may be available upon request; unauthorized late check-out may incur a fee of one additional night's rate.
  • All properties use self check-in via a keypad or lockbox. Access codes are sent 24 hours before arrival via the contact details provided at booking.
  • Please confirm your access code before arriving. If you experience any issue, call or text us at 801-657-1028.
05
House Rules

These rules apply to all Lake City Flats properties. Violations may result in immediate termination of your stay without refund and/or additional charges.

  • No smoking of any kind inside the property. This includes cigarettes, cigars, and vaping devices. Smoking is only permitted outside, away from doors and windows. A $250 cleaning fee applies for violations.
  • No pets unless explicitly stated otherwise in the individual property listing. An unauthorized pet fee of $150 per pet applies.
  • No parties or events. Guests and visitors combined may not exceed the listed maximum occupancy. Quiet hours are 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM in all neighborhoods.
  • Maximum occupancy is listed per property. Exceeding occupancy may result in immediate removal and forfeiture of your booking.
  • Treat the property with respect. Report any damage or maintenance issues immediately. Do not attempt to conceal damage — we'd rather know.
  • Parking (where available) is limited to the designated space(s) listed in your property details.
  • Do not leave doors unlocked or windows open when you are away from the property.
06
Damage & Security

Guests are responsible for any damage to the property, its furnishings, or its contents that occurs during their stay beyond normal wear and tear. We do not charge a security deposit upfront; instead, by completing your booking you authorize us to charge your payment method on file for any documented damages.

We will photograph and document all damage and provide you with an itemized summary before initiating any charge. If you dispute a damage claim, contact us within 48 hours of check-out.

$5M Liability Protection
All Lake City Flats properties carry comprehensive general liability coverage. Guests are covered for personal injury and property damage claims up to $5,000,000 during their stay.
07
Alterations & Modifications

Changes to confirmed reservations (date changes, property swaps) are subject to availability and may incur a price difference. Please contact us to discuss modifications before the original cancellation deadline to preserve your refund eligibility.

We reserve the right to relocate a guest to a comparable property in the event of an unforeseen issue with the reserved unit (e.g., emergency maintenance, plumbing failure). If a comparable property is not available and you do not wish to stay, you will receive a full refund.

08
Liability & Disclaimer

Lake City Flats, LLC shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from your stay, including but not limited to lost business, travel disruption, or personal injury resulting from guest negligence.

We make every effort to ensure properties are accurately represented, clean, and in good working order. If you arrive and find a condition that materially differs from the listing, notify us within 2 hours of check-in so we can address it.

We are not responsible for items left behind at the property. If you believe you've left something, contact us within 48 hours of check-out and we will make a reasonable effort to locate and return it. Shipping costs are the guest's responsibility.

09
Privacy

Properties may use exterior security cameras in common areas and building access points. No cameras are installed inside any property.
