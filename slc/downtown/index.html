Why downtown SLC?

Downtown Salt Lake City puts you within walking distance of everything — Delta Center for Jazz games and concerts, Temple Square and City Creek for shopping, the Eccles Theater for shows, and dozens of the city's best restaurants. TRAX light rail runs from the doorstep to the airport in 20 minutes. When it snows, the ski resorts are 30 minutes away — and you're already packed.
